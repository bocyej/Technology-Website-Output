 GOOGLE


 

Google is an internet search engine.  It uses an algorithm that is desgined to retrieve search results to provide the most relevant and dependable sources of data possible.  It's main purpose is to organize the information and make it accessible to the public.  Google is also the top search engine in the world, as it has millions of users and generated a flow of online information.



  Why is Google amazing?

Google first began as a search project by Larry Page and Sergey Brin when they were students.  Now, it is widely used by users all aroung the world.  The algorithm of the search engine is designed in a way that studies your interests and it shows contents related to it.  Most Google applications are very useful to people according to their needs, for example, Google Maps is useful for people who want to travel to an unknown location and makes it possible to them to arrive at their destination.  And also, most of Google's services are free-to-use!
