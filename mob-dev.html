 MOBILE DEVICES

Mobile devices is a general term for any type of handheld computer.  Tablets, smartphones, e-readers, PDAs, portable music players, smartwatches, and fitness trackers with smart capabilities are all examples of mobile devices.  These devices are designed to be extremely portable, and they can often fit in your hand.  Most mobile devices nowadays are powerful enough to do many of the same things you can do with a computer.



  Phone

Smartphones are a more powerful and advanced version of a traditional cell phone.  It has the same features as cellphones, such as the ability to make and receive phone calls, text message, and voicemail.  These smartphones offer additional features which you would normally do on a computer, like connecting to the Internet over Wi-Fi or cellular network and browsing the internet, send and receive emails, social media, shop online, etc.  Most smartphones also use a touch-sensitive screen, having a virtual keyboard and using your fingers to interact with the display.  Additional features of a smartphone include a high-quality camera, ability ot play music and video files, etc.  Smartphones can do what laptops, music players, and cameras can offer, but all within the palm of our hands.


  Tablet

Tablets are designed to be portable like laptops, but provide a different computing experience.  Their difference between laptops is that tablets don't have physical keyboards or touchpads, but instead it's entire screen is touch sensitive, having a virtual keyboard and uses your finger as a mouse pointer.  Another difference is that it runs apps designed specifically for tablets.  Modern tablets come in different sizes, ranging from a slightly larger size of a smartphone to a size of a small laptop.  Some tablets offer a keyboard and stylus accessory which some people may prefer than using virtual keyboard.  The main purpose of a tablet is to become a convenience when using as a second computer, as it cannot necessarily do everything traditional computers can do.



  Why are Mobile Devices amazing?

In the past, mobile devices aren't very mobile and requires a strong arm and back to carry them around.  Older mobile devices also always had to be beside a power outlet as batteries were not yet implemented.  Nowadays, mobile devices has improved a lot, and these devices have gotten smaller, more powerful, and more useful.  Mobile devices helps communication easier.  Its features such as the camera, which lets us capture memories and have video chats with our loved ones; smart healthcare capabilities, which lets people know another person's well-being; easier access to information, which makes us learn and gather information within the palm of our hands; and much more.  Mobile technology impacts our daily lives and helps us improve how we spend our day-to-day lives.
