 TECHNOLOGY

Technology refers to methods, systems, and devices which are the result of scientific knowledge being used for practical purposes.  It affects how individuals communicate, learn, and think. Technology helps society and determines how people interact with each other on a daily basis.  As it plays an important role in our lives, it can pose either a positive or negative effect on the world and impacts daily lives.  Technological advances are common in the era we live in today.  Some examples of these are the internet, smartphones, computers, etc.

An aspect of technology is that it greatly affects learning.  It made learning more interactive and collaborative through the use of the internet and our devices, and helps people better engage with the material that they are leaning and have trouble with.  People can also access information easier and faster as the creation of the internet provides us access to information to almost anything online.  Teachers can hold online classes, and students can finish their activities easier and faster, and can take quizzes and exams more effectively.  Online research can encourage not just students but also other people to have self-paced learning.  People can also gain information through videos and articles online.  This helps students learn better than sitting down for lectures and reading from textbooks.  These technological advancements made learning more fun and convenient.

Another way of technology has impacted society is through communication, how we talk and communicate with one another.  Technology brought new methods of electronic communication, for example, emails, text message, social networking, video call, etc.
